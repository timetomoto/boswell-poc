## “The Boswell Sisters: Close Harmony”

### Documentary Trailer

Scott and McCain brought us “Sing Sister Sing .Ross and Hampton brought us “The Boswell Sisters” via “Heebie Jeebies.” Now, filmmaker Dan Garrison brings us a sneak peek of “The Boswell Sisters: Close Harmony” . The trailer, now posted on You Tube, features narrative from Vet Boswell’s daughter, Chica, and Boswell historian David McCain, as well as a wealth of family photographs and film clips. The trailer has been produced by Joshua Tree Films, whose work has appeared on PBS, HBO, the History Channel, the Travel Channel, truTV, and in syndication.

When will this Bozumentary be completed? Stay tuned to Bozzies.org and we will keep you posted!
