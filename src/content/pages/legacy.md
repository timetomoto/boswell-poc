## Everybody Loves the Bozzies

The Judds, The Ditty Bops, Ella Fitzgerald, Judy Garland, The Andrews Sisters — the pantheon of artists who credit the Boswell Sisters as their inspiration goes on and on. This section gathers the community: tribute performers still playing the Boswell songbook, the Bozcasts audio series, ASCAP-honored scholarship, and the monthly Boz of the Month features that celebrated the fans who kept the flame lit.
