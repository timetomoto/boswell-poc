# Donate to the Boz Cause

We hope you are enjoying your visit. Bozzies.org is a non-profit, dedicated to keeping the memory of the Boswell Sisters alive. If you'd like to make a donation to help keep us Bozzing, we would be much obliged. But feel free to stay around and visit as often as you'd like — and maybe even pass the word along to a friend. We regularly add new content as we find it. If you know of any, please feel free to drop us a line.
